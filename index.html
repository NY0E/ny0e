About

Licensed ham and tinkerer building tools for the amateur radio community. Before changing careers into tech, I spent over a decade in education, working in curriculum design, training, and as a Professor of Political Science. I now bring that background to AI-assisted learning design, with the aim of closing the gap between passing the exam and actually knowing what you're doing.

Projects
QRZReady
Ham radio exam prep built around a simple principle: maximize exposure to correct answers, minimize exposure to wrong ones. Starts with one answer, gradually introduces distractors.
qrzready.com
CWReady
Audio-first CW trainer using the Koch method and Farnsworth spacing. Starts with your own callsign. Forced-choice recognition drills — no counting dots and dashes.
cwready.com
Elmer
Socratic AI tutor for newly licensed hams. Doesn't explain things — asks questions until you figure it out yourself. Built for operational knowledge, not exam prep.
elmer.ny0e.com
